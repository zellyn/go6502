** Differences from manual
The manual claims that "MSB ON" is the default.

:ASM FOO.TXT
SOURCE   FILE #01 =>FOO.TXT
0000:41 42 43        1           ASC   "ABC"
0003:                2           MSB   OFF
0003:41 42 43        3           ASC   "ABC"
0006:                4           MSB   ON
0006:C1 C2 C3        5           ASC   "ABC"
